Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of bread
  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place a slice of bread, butter side down, onto the skillet.
  4. Layer with cheese, bacon, tomato slices, and another slice of bread, butter side up.
  5. Cook until golden brown on one side, then flip and cook until cheese is melted and bread is golden brown.
  6. Repeat with remaining ingredients to make more sandwiches.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
